Lasagne

Lasagna worthy of giving a try

  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 2 hrs 30 mins
  • Additional Time: 15 mins
  • Total Time: 3 hrs 15 mins
Ingredients
  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup water, divided
  • 1½ t dried basil leaves
  • 1½ t salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 t oregano
  • ½ t fennel seeds, crushed
  • ¼ t ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1 c grated Parmesan cheese
Directions
  • -Cook onion in olive oil until translucent, add garlic and cook 2 min. Remove from pan.
  • -Cook sausage and ground beef over medium heat until well browned.
  • -Stir in onions, garlic, crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and ½ c water.
  • -Add basil, 1 teaspoon salt, oregano, fennel, and pepper.
  • -Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat.
  • -stir in ¼ c water.
  • -In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • -Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
To Assemble:
  • -spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  • Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce.
  • Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture, make sure pasta is covered completely.
  • Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
  • Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella.
  • Sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  • Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  • -Cover with foil. To prevent sticking, spray foil with cooking spray.
  • -Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes.
  • Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.

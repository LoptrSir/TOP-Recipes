Keto Brussel Sprouts (keto)

Brussel sprouts in a creamy garlic parmesan sauce.

  • Prep Time 10 minutes
  • Cook Time 20 minutes
  • Servings 4 people
  • Calories 508cal
Ingredients
  • 6 slices bacon chopped fine
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 cups (or 32 oz) brussel sprouts bottoms removed and halved
  • ½ cup onion minced (optional)
  • 3-4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning optional
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese*
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese*
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
Directions

-Heat a skillet to medium-high heat. Add the bacon and cook 3-4 minutes or until crispy.

-Remove bacon from the pan, add 1 tablespoon butter, brussels sprouts, and onion. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning; Cook until sprouts start showing browning, stirring occasionally. Add the garlic and cook for another 2 minutes, cover with a lid or a bowl and let the sprouts steam till fork tender.

-Add heavy cream, stir until simmering. Reduce heat to med-low. Taste and adjust for salt and pepper.

-Stir in cheese and bacon. Once melted, remove from heat and serve.

Notes

Cheese substitutions: Either cheese can be swapped out for gruyere, asiago, cheddar, or your favorite melting cheese.
